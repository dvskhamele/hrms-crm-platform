The Key Phases of Onboarding

Our checklist generator is built around the core components of a successful onboarding program. These categories ensure a holistic experience for your new employee.

1. IT Setup

Nothing is more frustrating for a new hire than not having the tools they need to do their job. This phase includes setting up their computer, creating necessary accounts (email, software, etc.), and ensuring they have access to all required systems on day one.

2. HR Paperwork

This covers all the essential administrative tasks. It includes signing employment contracts, filling out tax forms, and enrolling in benefits. Getting this done efficiently allows the new hire to focus on their role.

3. Team & Role Training

This is the core of onboarding. It involves clarifying the new hire's role and responsibilities, setting initial performance goals, introducing them to their team members, and providing access to training resources. Assigning an onboarding buddy or mentor is a crucial step in this phase.

4. Company Culture Introduction

This phase is about making the new hire feel welcome and part of the team. It includes activities like a welcome lunch, an office tour, and an introduction to the company's mission, vision, and values. This helps them understand not just what they do, but why they do it.
