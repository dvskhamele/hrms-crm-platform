Optimize Your New Hire Experience with Gem's Employee Onboarding Feedback Form Generator

A successful onboarding process is critical for new hire engagement, productivity, and retention. Gathering feedback from new employees is the best way to continuously improve this vital process. Gem HRMS is excited to introduce the Employee Onboarding Feedback Form Generator, a free, frontend-only JavaScript tool designed to help HR professionals and managers create customized feedback forms to gather valuable insights from their newest team members.

The Value of Onboarding Feedback

Effective onboarding feedback helps organizations to:

  • Improve New Hire Experience: Address pain points and enhance the overall journey for new employees.
  • Increase Retention: Identify and resolve issues that might lead to early turnover.
  • Boost Productivity: Ensure new hires are quickly integrated and equipped for success.
  • Refine HR Processes: Continuously optimize onboarding programs based on real-world feedback.

Introducing Gem's Employee Onboarding Feedback Form Generator

The Employee Onboarding Feedback Form Generator is an intuitive tool that allows you to create customized feedback forms tailored to your specific onboarding process. You can specify the department, onboarding duration, and even include a section for manager feedback, ensuring you capture the most relevant insights.

How It Works: Create Tailored Feedback Forms in Minutes

  1. Define Parameters: Enter the department, onboarding duration, and choose to include manager feedback.
  2. Generate Form: Instantly receive a comprehensive feedback form with relevant questions.
  3. Gather Insights: Use the generated form to collect valuable feedback and improve your onboarding.

Operating entirely client-side, this tool ensures that your sensitive onboarding data remains private and secure within your browser.

Key Features and Benefits

  • Customizable Questions: Tailor forms to specific departments and onboarding phases.
  • Manager Feedback Option: Include sections for feedback on manager support during onboarding.
  • Time-Saving: Quickly create professional feedback forms without starting from scratch.
  • Data Privacy: All processing happens in your browser, keeping your data confidential.
  • 100% Free & Accessible: No sign-up required to use the generator.
