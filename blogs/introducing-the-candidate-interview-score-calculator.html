What is the Candidate Interview Score Calculator?

This tool provides a standardized way to score candidates across various criteria during an interview. By assigning numerical values to key skills and attributes, you can reduce bias, facilitate clearer comparisons between candidates, and make more informed hiring decisions.

How to Use the Tool

Using the Candidate Interview Score Calculator is straightforward:

  1. Define Criteria: The tool comes with predefined criteria such as Technical Skills, Problem Solving, Communication, Teamwork, Experience, and Cultural Fit.
  2. Assign Scores: During or immediately after an interview, assign a score (e.g., 0-10) to the candidate for each criterion.
  3. Calculate Total: Click the "Calculate Score" button to get an instant total score for the candidate.
  4. Compare and Decide: Use the scores to compare candidates objectively and support your hiring discussions.

Benefits of Using a Score Calculator

  • Reduces Bias: Standardized scoring minimizes subjective judgments.
  • Improves Consistency: Ensures all candidates are evaluated against the same benchmarks.
  • Facilitates Discussion: Provides concrete data points for post-interview debriefs.
  • Data-Driven Decisions: Supports a more analytical approach to hiring.
