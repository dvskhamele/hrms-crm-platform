Why Use an Interview Scorecard?

Interview scorecards provide a structured framework for evaluating candidates. By defining key criteria and a consistent scoring system before an interview, you can:

  • Reduce Unconscious Bias: Focus on job-related skills and qualifications rather than gut feelings.
  • Improve Consistency: Ensure every candidate is evaluated on the same metrics, making comparisons fair and simple.
  • Enhance Collaboration: Give your hiring team a common language and framework for discussing candidates.
  • Make Data-Driven Decisions: Justify your hiring choices with concrete data, not just intuition.
