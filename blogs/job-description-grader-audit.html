Your job description is often the first impression a candidate has of your company and the role. A well-crafted job description can attract top talent, while a poor one can deter qualified applicants. To help you optimize this crucial document, we're excited to introduce our new Job Description Grader.

Why Grade Your Job Descriptions?

An effective job description is clear, concise, inclusive, and accurately reflects the role. Grading your job descriptions helps you to:

  • Attract Better Candidates: Ensure your descriptions resonate with the talent you want to hire.
  • Improve Inclusivity: Identify and remove biased language that might discourage diverse applicants.
  • Enhance Clarity: Make sure the responsibilities and requirements are easy to understand.
  • Save Time: Reduce the number of unqualified applications by setting clear expectations.

How the Job Description Grader Works

Our tool provides instant feedback and actionable insights:

  1. Paste Your Job Description: Simply copy and paste your existing job description into the text area.
  2. Grade Description: The tool will analyze your text based on several criteria, including:
    • Inclusive Language: Checks for gender-neutral terms and inclusive phrasing.
    • Action Verbs: Evaluates the use of strong, descriptive verbs to define responsibilities.
    • Readability: Assesses how easy your description is to read and understand.
  3. Receive a Grade and Feedback: Get an overall grade and specific suggestions for improvement.
