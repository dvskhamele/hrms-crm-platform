Understanding the true cost of hiring is fundamental for any HR or talent acquisition team. Our new Recruitment Cost Per Hire Calculator provides a clear, data-driven way to measure the efficiency of your recruitment process and optimize your spending.

Why Calculate Cost Per Hire (CPH)?

CPH is a critical metric that helps you to:

  • Benchmark Performance: Compare your hiring efficiency against industry standards or internal goals.
  • Optimize Budget Allocation: Identify which recruitment channels and strategies offer the best value.
  • Demonstrate ROI: Quantify the financial impact of your talent acquisition efforts to stakeholders.
  • Improve Decision-Making: Make informed choices about where to invest your recruitment resources.

How the Recruitment Cost Per Hire Calculator Works

Our tool simplifies the calculation of this key metric:

  1. Total Internal Recruitment Costs: Include expenses like recruiter salaries, benefits, and internal team overhead.
  2. Total External Recruitment Costs: Account for external costs such as job board fees, agency fees, background checks, and advertising.
  3. Total Number of Hires: Input the total number of successful hires made during the period you're analyzing.
  4. Calculate CPH: The tool will instantly provide your average cost per hire.
