Introducing the Team Cohesion Assessment

We're excited to launch our new Team Cohesion Assessment tool. This tool is designed to help you measure how well your team works together and identify areas for improvement.

Why is Team Cohesion Important?

A cohesive team is a productive team. When team members are able to work together effectively, they are more likely to achieve their goals, innovate, and support each other. A cohesive team is also more resilient in the face of challenges.

How it Works

The Team Cohesion Assessment is a short quiz that asks team members a series of questions about their team's communication, trust, conflict resolution, and commitment. Based on their answers, the tool calculates a team cohesion score and provides a cohesion level (low, moderate, or high).

This tool will help you:

  • Gain insights into your team's dynamics.
  • Identify areas where your team can improve.
  • Take steps to build a stronger, more cohesive team.
