The Impact of Effective Recognition

When employees feel valued and appreciated, they are more productive, committed, and satisfied with their jobs. A well-structured recognition program can significantly enhance workplace culture, reduce turnover, and foster a positive environment. But what works for a small startup might not be suitable for a large corporation, and budget constraints always play a role.
