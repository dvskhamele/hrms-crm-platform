The Hidden Costs of Employee Turnover

Many organizations only consider the direct costs of hiring a replacement. However, the full cost of turnover includes a wide range of factors:

  • Recruitment Costs: Advertising, screening, interviewing, background checks.
  • Onboarding & Training: New hire orientation, training programs, materials, trainer's time.
  • Lost Productivity: Reduced output from the departing employee, vacant position, and ramp-up time for the new hire.
  • Management Time: Time spent by managers on exit interviews, hiring, and training.
  • Decreased Morale: Impact on remaining team members.
  • Loss of Institutional Knowledge: Valuable experience and expertise walking out the door.

Our calculator brings these hidden costs to light, enabling more informed strategic decisions.

How to Use the Tool (Step-by-Step Guide)

  1. Navigate to the Employee Turnover Cost Calculator.
  2. Enter the Employee Annual Salary for the position.
  3. Input the Benefits as % of Salary (e.g., 25 for 25%).
  4. Provide the estimated Recruitment Costs (e.g., advertising, agency fees).
  5. Enter the Onboarding & Training Costs for a new hire.
  6. Estimate the Lost Productivity Days until a new hire is fully productive.
