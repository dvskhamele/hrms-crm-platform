Introducing the Cost Per Hire Calculator

We're excited to launch our new Cost Per Hire Calculator. This tool is designed to help you calculate your cost per hire, a key metric for measuring the efficiency of your recruitment process.

Why Calculate Cost Per Hire?

Calculating your cost per hire can help you to understand the total investment you are making in your recruitment efforts. This can help you to identify areas where you can save money, optimize your recruitment process, and make more informed decisions about your hiring strategy.

How it Works

The Cost Per Hire Calculator is a simple tool that allows you to calculate your cost per hire by entering your internal and external recruitment costs, and the number of hires you have made. The tool will then calculate your cost per hire and display it on the screen.

This tool will help you:

  • Calculate your cost per hire quickly and easily.
  • Track your recruitment costs over time.
  • Make more informed decisions about your hiring strategy.
