How to Use

  1. Define Criteria: Start by identifying the key skills, competencies, and qualifications essential for the role.
  2. Set Scoring Scale: Choose a consistent scoring scale (e.g., 1-5, or "Does Not Meet" to "Exceeds Expectations").
  3. Generate Scorecard: Our tool will help you structure these into a clear, printable scorecard.
  4. Evaluate Candidates: Use the generated scorecard during interviews to objectively rate candidates against predefined criteria.
  5. Compare and Decide: Easily compare candidate scores to make data-driven hiring decisions.
