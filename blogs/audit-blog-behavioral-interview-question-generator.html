The Power of Behavioral Interviewing

Behavioral interviewing is based on the premise that past behavior is the best predictor of future performance. By asking candidates to describe how they handled specific situations, you gain a deeper understanding of their competencies, problem-solving abilities, and cultural fit. However, developing effective behavioral questions can be challenging.
