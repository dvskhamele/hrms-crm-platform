Introducing the Change Management Impact Assessment

We're excited to launch our new Change Management Impact Assessment tool. This tool is designed to help you assess the potential impact of a change on your organization, so you can plan and manage the change effectively.

Why Assess the Impact of Change?

Change is inevitable, but it doesn't have to be disruptive. By assessing the impact of a change before you implement it, you can identify potential risks and challenges, and develop a plan to mitigate them. This can help you ensure a smooth and successful transition for everyone involved.

How it Works

The Change Management Impact Assessment is a short quiz that asks you a series of questions about the change you are planning. Based on your answers, the tool calculates a change impact score and provides an impact level (low, medium, or high).

This tool will help you:

  • Understand the potential impact of a change on your organization.
  • Identify potential risks and challenges.
  • Develop a plan to manage the change effectively.
