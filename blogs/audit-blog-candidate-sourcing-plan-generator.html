The Importance of a Strategic Sourcing Plan

Effective sourcing goes beyond simply posting job ads. It involves identifying target candidate profiles, understanding where they can be found, and developing tailored strategies to engage them. A well-defined sourcing plan ensures that your recruitment efforts are focused, efficient, and yield high-quality candidates, reducing time-to-hire and cost-per-hire.

How to Use the Generator

Our Candidate Sourcing Plan Generator is designed for ease of use:

  1. Enter Job Title and Target Seniority: Define the role you are sourcing for and the experience level of your ideal candidate.
  2. Add Key Skills/Requirements: List the essential skills, qualifications, and experiences necessary for the position.
  3. Add Sourcing Channels: Specify the platforms and methods you plan to use to find candidates (e.g., LinkedIn Recruiter, industry-specific job boards, employee referral programs, professional networks).
  4. Generate Sourcing Plan: The tool will instantly produce a basic outline of your sourcing plan, consolidating your inputs into a clear, actionable document.
