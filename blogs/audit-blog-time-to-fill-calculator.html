Audit Blog: Time to Fill Calculator

The Time to Fill Calculator is a simple tool that helps HR professionals and recruiters calculate the number of days it takes to fill a job position.

How to Use

### Understanding Time to Fill

Time to fill is a crucial metric in recruitment, measuring the efficiency of your hiring process from job requisition to candidate acceptance. A shorter time to fill often indicates a more agile and effective recruitment strategy.
