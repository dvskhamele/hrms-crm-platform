What is the Time-to-Hire Calculator?

This simple yet powerful tool allows you to quickly calculate the time-to-hire for any position. By inputting the job posted date and the offer accepted date, you can instantly determine the number of days it took to fill the role, providing valuable insights into your recruitment process efficiency.

How to Use the Tool

Calculating time-to-hire is straightforward:

  1. Enter Job Posted Date: Select the date when the job advertisement was first published.
  2. Enter Offer Accepted Date: Select the date when the chosen candidate formally accepted the job offer.
  3. Calculate Time-to-Hire: Click the "Calculate Time-to-Hire" button.
  4. Review Result: The tool will display the total number of days taken to fill the position.

  • Reduce Costs: Faster hiring can minimize the costs associated with vacant positions.
