Effective recruitment requires not only a strong strategy but also a well-managed budget. To help HR and talent acquisition teams plan and control their spending, we're excited to launch our new Recruitment Budget Calculator.

Why a Dedicated Recruitment Budget Calculator?

Recruitment costs can quickly add up, from job postings and agency fees to assessment tools and onboarding. A specialized calculator helps you to:

  • Forecast Expenses: Accurately estimate the total cost of your hiring initiatives.
  • Optimize Spending: Identify areas where you can allocate resources more efficiently.
  • Justify Investments: Present clear financial projections to stakeholders.
  • Track Performance: Compare planned vs. actual spending to refine future budgets.

How the Recruitment Budget Calculator Works

Our tool is designed for simplicity and comprehensive planning:

  1. Number of Hires Needed: Input the total number of positions you plan to fill.
  2. Average Cost Per Hire: Enter your estimated or historical average cost to hire a single employee.
  3. Marketing & Advertising Budget: Allocate funds for job board postings, social media campaigns, and other advertising efforts.
  4. Tools & Software Cost: Include expenses for ATS, CRM, assessment platforms, and other recruitment software.
  5. Calculate Budget: The tool will provide a summary of your estimated total recruitment budget.
