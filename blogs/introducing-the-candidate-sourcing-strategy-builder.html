Introducing the Candidate Sourcing Strategy Builder

We're excited to launch our new Candidate Sourcing Strategy Builder. This tool is designed to help HR professionals and recruiters develop tailored sourcing strategies to attract the best talent for any role, seniority level, and location.

Why is a Strategic Sourcing Approach Essential?

In today's competitive talent landscape, a generic approach to candidate sourcing is no longer sufficient. A strategic sourcing strategy ensures you're looking in the right places, using the most effective channels, and reaching the candidates who are the best fit for your roles. This leads to higher quality hires, reduced time-to-fill, and a stronger talent pipeline.

How it Works

The Candidate Sourcing Strategy Builder is a simple tool that allows you to input the role type, seniority level, and location. Based on these inputs, the tool generates a list of recommended sourcing channels, including general platforms like LinkedIn and Indeed, as well as more specialized options like executive search firms for senior roles or university career fairs for entry-level positions. It also considers location-specific factors to provide a comprehensive strategy.

This tool will help you:

  • Develop customized and effective candidate sourcing strategies.
  • Identify the most relevant channels to find top talent.
  • Optimize your recruitment efforts and resource allocation.
  • Improve the quality and diversity of your candidate pipeline.
