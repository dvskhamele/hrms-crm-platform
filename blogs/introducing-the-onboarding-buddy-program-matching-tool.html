Introducing the Onboarding Buddy Program Matching Tool

We're excited to launch our new Onboarding Buddy Program Matching Tool. This tool is designed to help you match new hires with onboarding buddies, so you can provide a more supportive and engaging onboarding experience.

Why Use an Onboarding Buddy Program?

An onboarding buddy program can help new hires to feel more welcome and supported during their first few weeks and months on the job. Buddies can provide guidance, answer questions, and help new hires to navigate the company culture. This can lead to a more positive onboarding experience, faster ramp-up time, and higher retention rates.

How it Works

The Onboarding Buddy Program Matching Tool is a simple tool that allows you to match new hires with onboarding buddies. Simply enter the name of the new hire and the name of the buddy, and the tool will generate a confirmation message.

This tool will help you:

  • Match new hires with onboarding buddies quickly and easily.
  • Provide a more supportive and engaging onboarding experience.
  • Improve new hire retention and engagement.
