Development Cycle Report

Summary of Work

This development cycle focused on further expanding the suite of free HR tools and enhancing the PSEO content strategy. The primary achievement was the creation and integration of a new 'Employee Onboarding Feedback Form Generator' tool.

New Tool: Employee Onboarding Feedback Form Generator

A new tool, the 'Employee Onboarding Feedback Form Generator', was developed and deployed. This tool helps HR professionals and managers create customized feedback forms to gather valuable insights from new hires, optimizing the onboarding process and improving new hire retention.

  • Tool Files Created:
    • /tools/employee-onboarding-feedback-form-generator/index.html
    • /tools/employee-onboarding-feedback-form-generator/style.css
    • /tools/employee-onboarding-feedback-form-generator/script.js
    • /tools/results/employee-onboarding-feedback-form-generator-results.html
  • Functionality:
    • Frontend-only, client-side processing for privacy and speed.
    • Simple UI for ease of use.
    • Generates a shareable results page with the generated feedback form.
    • Includes a lead capture form for best practices on collecting and acting on onboarding feedback.
    • Analytics events for tracking tool usage (ToolOpened, ToolSubmitted, ToolResultViewed, LeadCaptured).

PSEO Content and Integration

    • Added a link to the new tool in the footer of the homepage.

Next Steps
