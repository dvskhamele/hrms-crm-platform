Development Cycle Report

Date: 2025-10-23 (Part 12)

Summary of Work

This development cycle focused on further expanding the suite of free HR tools and enhancing the PSEO content strategy. The primary achievement was the creation and integration of a new 'Job Offer Letter Generator' tool.

New Tool: Job Offer Letter Generator

A new tool, the 'Job Offer Letter Generator', was developed and deployed. This tool helps HR professionals and hiring managers quickly create customized and professional job offer letters, streamlining the hiring process and impressing top candidates.

  • Tool Files Created:
    • /tools/job-offer-letter-generator/index.html
    • /tools/job-offer-letter-generator/style.css
    • /tools/job-offer-letter-generator/script.js
    • /tools/results/job-offer-letter-generator-results.html
  • Functionality:
    • Frontend-only, client-side processing for privacy and speed.
    • Simple UI for ease of use.
    • Generates a shareable results page with the customized offer letter.
    • Includes a lead capture form for comprehensive offer letter templates.
    • Analytics events for tracking tool usage (ToolOpened, ToolSubmitted, ToolResultViewed, LeadCaptured).

PSEO Content and Integration

To support the new tool and drive organic traffic, a new blog post was created.

  • Blog Post Created:
    • /blogs/job-offer-letter-generator.html
  • Homepage Integration:
    • Added a link to the new tool in the 'Our Free HR Tools' section of the homepage.
    • Added a link to the new tool in the footer of the homepage.

Next Steps

The next development cycle will focus on creating another new tool and continuing to expand the PSEO content library.
