Development Cycle Report

Date: 2025-10-23

Summary of Work

This development cycle focused on expanding the suite of free HR tools and enhancing the PSEO content strategy. The primary achievement was the creation and integration of a new 'Salary Range Estimator' tool.

New Tool: Salary Range Estimator

A new tool, the 'Salary Range Estimator', was developed and deployed. This tool allows HR professionals and hiring managers to get a data-driven estimate of a competitive salary range for a given role, based on job title, experience level, location, and industry.

  • Tool Files Created:
    • /tools/salary-range-estimator/index.html
    • /tools/salary-range-estimator/style.css
    • /tools/salary-range-estimator/script.js
    • /tools/results/salary-range-estimator-results.html
  • Functionality:
    • Frontend-only, client-side calculation for privacy and speed.
    • Simple UI for ease of use.
    • Generates a shareable results page with a unique URL.
    • Includes a lead capture form for a detailed report.
    • Analytics events for tracking tool usage (ToolOpened, ToolSubmitted, ToolResultViewed, LeadCaptured).

PSEO Content and Integration

To support the new tool and drive organic traffic, a new blog post was created.

  • Blog Post Created:
    • /blogs/salary-range-estimator.html
  • Homepage Integration:
    • Added a link to the new tool in the 'Our Free HR Tools' section of the homepage.
    • Added a link to the new tool in the footer of the homepage.

Next Steps

The next development cycle will focus on creating another new tool, likely a 'Recruitment ROI Calculator', and continuing to expand the PSEO content library.
