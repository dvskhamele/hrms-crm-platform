Development Cycle Report

Summary of Work

This development cycle focused on further expanding the suite of free HR tools and enhancing the PSEO content strategy. The primary achievement was the creation and integration of a new 'Employee Onboarding Checklist Generator' tool.

New Tool: Employee Onboarding Checklist Generator

A new tool, the 'Employee Onboarding Checklist Generator', was developed and deployed. This tool helps HR professionals and managers quickly create customized onboarding checklists to ensure a smooth and efficient start for every new team member, covering general tasks, IT setup, and HR paperwork.

  • Tool Files Created:
    • /tools/employee-onboarding-checklist-generator/index.html
    • /tools/employee-onboarding-checklist-generator/style.css
    • /tools/employee-onboarding-checklist-generator/script.js
    • /tools/results/employee-onboarding-checklist-generator-results.html
  • Functionality:
    • Frontend-only, client-side processing for privacy and speed.
    • Simple UI for ease of use.
    • Generates a shareable results page with the customized checklist.
    • Includes a lead capture form for comprehensive onboarding resources.
    • Analytics events for tracking tool usage (ToolOpened, ToolSubmitted, ToolResultViewed, LeadCaptured).
