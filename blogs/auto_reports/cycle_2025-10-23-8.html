Summary of Work

This development cycle focused on further expanding the suite of free HR tools and enhancing the PSEO content strategy. The primary achievement was the creation and integration of a new 'Employee Performance Review Template Generator' tool.

New Tool: Employee Performance Review Template Generator

A new tool, the 'Employee Performance Review Template Generator', was developed and deployed. This tool helps HR professionals and managers quickly create customized review templates that streamline evaluations, provide constructive feedback, and foster employee growth.

  • Tool Files Created:
    • /tools/employee-performance-review-template-generator/index.html
    • /tools/employee-performance-review-template-generator/style.css
    • /tools/employee-performance-review-template-generator/script.js
    • /tools/results/employee-performance-review-template-generator-results.html
  • Functionality:
    • Frontend-only, client-side processing for privacy and speed.
    • Simple UI for ease of use.
    • Generates a shareable results page with the generated template.
    • Includes a lead capture form for advanced performance management resources.
    • Analytics events for tracking tool usage (ToolOpened, ToolSubmitted, ToolResultViewed, LeadCaptured).
