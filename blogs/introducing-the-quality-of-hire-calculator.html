Introducing the Quality of Hire Calculator

We're excited to launch our new Quality of Hire Calculator. This tool is designed to help you measure the quality of your hires, a key metric for evaluating the success of your recruitment process.

Why Calculate Quality of Hire?

Calculating your quality of hire can help you to understand the value that new hires bring to your organization. This can help you to identify your most successful recruitment sources, improve your selection process, and make more informed decisions about your hiring strategy.

How it Works

The Quality of Hire Calculator is a simple tool that allows you to calculate your quality of hire by entering data on new hire performance, retention, and ramp-up time. The tool will then calculate your quality of hire and display it on the screen.

This tool will help you:

  • Calculate your quality of hire quickly and easily.
  • Track the quality of your hires over time.
  • Make more informed decisions about your hiring strategy.
