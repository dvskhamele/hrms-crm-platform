What is eNPS?

The Employee Net Promoter Score (eNPS) is a metric used to measure employee loyalty and engagement. It is based on a single question: "On a scale of 0-10, how likely are you to recommend our company as a place to work?" Based on their responses, employees are categorized as promoters (9-10), passives (7-8), or detractors (0-6).

How it Works

The eNPS Calculator makes it easy to calculate your eNPS score. Simply enter the number of promoters, passives, and detractors, and the tool will do the rest. The eNPS score is calculated by subtracting the percentage of detractors from the percentage of promoters.

This tool will help you:

  • Quickly and easily calculate your eNPS score.
  • Track employee loyalty and engagement over time.
  • Identify areas for improvement in the employee experience.
