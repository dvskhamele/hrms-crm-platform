The Importance of a Strategic Onboarding Plan

Effective onboarding goes beyond just paperwork; it's about making new employees feel welcomed, supported, and equipped to succeed. A strategic onboarding plan reduces ramp-up time, increases engagement, and significantly improves retention rates. Without a clear plan, new hires can feel lost, leading to early attrition and wasted resources.
