Introducing the Sourcing Channel Effectiveness Calculator

We're excited to launch our new Sourcing Channel Effectiveness Calculator. This tool is designed to help you measure the effectiveness of your sourcing channels, so you can optimize your recruitment strategy and find the best candidates.

Why Measure Sourcing Channel Effectiveness?

Measuring the effectiveness of your sourcing channels can help you to understand which channels are providing the best return on investment. This can help you to allocate your resources more effectively, improve the quality of your hires, and reduce your time to fill.

How it Works

The Sourcing Channel Effectiveness Calculator is a simple tool that allows you to calculate the effectiveness of your sourcing channels by entering the number of applications, interviews, and hires for each channel. The tool will then calculate your application-to-interview rate, interview-to-hire rate, and application-to-hire rate.

This tool will help you:

  • Calculate the effectiveness of your sourcing channels quickly and easily.
  • Track the performance of your sourcing channels over time.
  • Make more informed decisions about your recruitment strategy.
