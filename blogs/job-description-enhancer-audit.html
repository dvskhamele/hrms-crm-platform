Why Your Job Descriptions Matter

In a competitive job market, a poorly written job description can deter qualified candidates. Common pitfalls include:

  • Vague Language: Failing to clearly articulate the role and responsibilities.
  • Non-Inclusive Wording: Using language that unintentionally discourages diverse candidates from applying.
  • Missing Key Information: Forgetting to include essential skills or qualifications.
