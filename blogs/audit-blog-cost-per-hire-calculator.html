What is Cost Per Hire?

Cost Per Hire (CPH) is a recruiting metric that measures the total amount of money an organization spends to recruit new employees. It includes all internal and external costs associated with hiring, divided by the number of hires made within a specific period. A lower CPH generally indicates a more efficient recruitment process.
