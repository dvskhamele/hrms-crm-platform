Why a Strategic Approach to Sourcing?

Simply posting a job opening is often not enough. A strategic sourcing approach allows you to:

  • Reach Passive Candidates: Tap into a wider pool of talent who aren't actively looking for jobs.
  • Improve Candidate Quality: Target individuals with specific skills and experience that align perfectly with your needs.
  • Reduce Time-to-Hire: Streamline your search process and fill critical roles faster.
  • Enhance Employer Brand: Proactively engage with potential candidates, building positive relationships.

How the Candidate Sourcing Strategy Builder Works

Our tool guides you through building a customized sourcing plan:

  1. Define the Role: Enter the job title, desired seniority level, and key skills required.
  2. Specify Location: Optionally, add location preferences (e.g., remote, specific city).
  3. Build Strategy: The tool generates a comprehensive strategy outlining recommended sourcing channels and engagement tactics.
