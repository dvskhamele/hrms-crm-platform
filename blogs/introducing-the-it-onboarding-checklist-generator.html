Key Phases of IT Onboarding

Our generator breaks down the IT onboarding process into critical phases, ensuring a comprehensive approach to tech readiness.

1. Pre-Arrival (Before Start Date)

This phase involves all preparatory steps before the new hire's first day. It includes creating user accounts (email, network, software), ordering and configuring hardware, installing software, setting up VPN access, and preparing an IT welcome packet with login credentials and support contacts.

2. First Day IT Setup

On the first day, the focus is on ensuring the new hire's equipment is fully functional. This includes conducting an IT orientation, assisting with hardware setup, verifying software installations and access, and reviewing IT security policies.

3. First Week IT Integration

During the first week, follow-up is crucial. This involves addressing any outstanding IT issues, ensuring access to role-specific applications, providing training on specialized software, and confirming understanding of password policies and multi-factor authentication.
