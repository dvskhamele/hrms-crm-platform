Introducing the Time to Fill Calculator

We're excited to launch our new Time to Fill Calculator. This tool is designed to help you calculate the time it takes to fill a job position, a key metric for measuring the efficiency of your recruitment process.

Why Calculate Time to Fill?

Calculating your time to fill can help you to understand how long it takes to find and hire a new employee. This can help you to identify bottlenecks in your recruitment process, set realistic expectations for hiring managers, and make more informed decisions about your hiring strategy.

How it Works

The Time to Fill Calculator is a simple tool that allows you to calculate your time to fill by entering the date a job requisition was opened and the date a candidate accepted an offer. The tool will then calculate your time to fill and display it on the screen.

This tool will help you:

  • Calculate your time to fill quickly and easily.
  • Track your recruitment efficiency over time.
  • Make more informed decisions about your hiring strategy.
