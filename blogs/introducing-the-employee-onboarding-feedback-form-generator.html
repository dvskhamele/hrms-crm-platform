Introducing the Employee Onboarding Feedback Form Generator

We're excited to launch our new Employee Onboarding Feedback Form Generator. This tool is designed to help HR professionals and managers create structured feedback forms to gather crucial insights from new hires about their onboarding experience, enabling continuous improvement and faster integration.

Why is Onboarding Feedback Essential?

The onboarding process is a critical period that significantly impacts a new employee's success and retention. Gathering feedback directly from new hires provides invaluable insights into the effectiveness of your onboarding program. It helps identify pain points, celebrate successes, and make data-driven adjustments to ensure every new employee feels welcomed, supported, and quickly integrated into the team and company culture. This proactive approach can drastically improve time-to-productivity and reduce early turnover.

How it Works

The Employee Onboarding Feedback Form Generator is a simple tool where you input the employee's name and their onboarding start date. The tool then generates a comprehensive feedback form template, structured with sections covering the pre-boarding experience, first week experience, training & resources, manager & team support, and overall onboarding experience. Each section includes rating scales and open-ended comment boxes, allowing for both quantitative and qualitative feedback.

This tool will help you:

  • Generate customized and comprehensive onboarding feedback forms quickly.
  • Systematically collect valuable insights from new hires.
  • Identify strengths and weaknesses in your current onboarding process.
  • Drive continuous improvement to enhance the new hire experience and retention.
