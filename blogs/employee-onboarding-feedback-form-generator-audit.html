A successful onboarding process is crucial for new hire retention and productivity. But how do you know if your onboarding is truly effective? By gathering feedback directly from your new employees! The Challenge of Unstructured Onboarding Feedback

Many organizations collect onboarding feedback informally, or use generic templates that don't capture specific insights relevant to their unique process. This can lead to missed opportunities for improvement, a lack of actionable data, and ultimately, a less effective onboarding experience for new hires. A structured and customizable approach to feedback collection is essential.

Introducing Gem's Employee Onboarding Feedback Form Generator

The **Employee Onboarding Feedback Form Generator** is an intuitive tool that streamlines the process of creating tailored feedback forms. By simply inputting key details and selecting relevant questions, you can instantly generate a professional and comprehensive feedback form.

How It Works: Generate Your Form in Three Steps

  1. **Define New Hire Details:** Enter the new hire's name, department, and onboarding start date.
  2. **Select & Add Questions:** Choose from a list of common onboarding questions and add your own custom questions.
  3. **Generate Form:** Instantly receive a ready-to-use feedback form that you can share with your new hires.

Operating entirely client-side, this tool ensures that your sensitive new hire details and feedback questions remain private and secure within your browser, never transmitted to external servers.
