What is the Candidate Sourcing Plan Generator?

This tool helps you create a tailored sourcing plan based on specific job roles and target demographics. It outlines key steps, channels, and strategies to identify, engage, and attract qualified candidates, ensuring a more efficient and successful recruitment process.

How to Use the Tool

Generating a candidate sourcing plan is simple:

  1. Enter Job Role: Input the specific job title you are recruiting for (e.g., Senior Software Engineer).
  2. Define Target Demographics: Describe the ideal candidate's profile, including experience level, key skills, and location preferences.
  3. Generate Sourcing Plan: Click the "Generate Sourcing Plan" button.
  4. Review and Implement: A comprehensive sourcing plan will be instantly displayed, providing actionable steps and channels to find your next great hire.
