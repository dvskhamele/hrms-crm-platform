Introducing the Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) Generator

We're excited to launch our new Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) Generator. This tool is designed to help managers create clear and actionable PIPs for employees who need to improve their performance.

Why Use a Performance Improvement Plan?

A Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) is a structured approach to help employees improve their performance. It provides a clear framework for identifying areas of concern, setting expectations, outlining action steps, and establishing a timeline for improvement. A well-designed PIP can help employees to succeed, while also protecting the company in the event that performance issues persist.

How it Works

The Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) Generator is a simple tool that allows you to generate a PIP by entering the employee's name, the area of concern, the desired outcome, action steps, and a timeline. The tool will then generate a comprehensive PIP that you can use to guide your conversations with employees.

This tool will help you:

  • Create clear and actionable PIPs quickly and easily.
  • Provide employees with a roadmap for improvement.
  • Document performance issues and improvement efforts.
