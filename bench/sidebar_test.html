Sidebar Test Page

This is a test page to verify that the sidebar is working correctly.

Sidebar Visibility

The sidebar should be visible on the left side of the screen on desktop and accessible via the hamburger menu on mobile.

If you can see this message and the sidebar, the test is successful!

Desktop View

On screens wider than 1024px, the sidebar should be permanently visible on the left side.

Resize your browser to test responsive behavior

Mobile View

On screens narrower than 1024px, the sidebar should be hidden and accessible via the hamburger menu.

Click the hamburger icon to open the sidebar

Test Instructions

  1. Check that the sidebar is visible on the left side of the screen on desktop
  2. Resize your browser to a mobile width (<1024px)
  3. Verify that the sidebar disappears and a hamburger menu appears in the top-left
  4. Click the hamburger menu to open the mobile sidebar
  5. Verify that the sidebar overlay appears and the sidebar slides in from the left
  6. Click the close button or overlay to close the mobile sidebar
